Audio

All of the audio in HyperStars are created by me in collaboration with Donovan Robinson, Sherry Jin, and Regina Xia. The goal for HyperStar's audio was to be as interactive as possible to best accompany the fast paced action in gameplay.

Wwise

I used Wwise for 2 major elements for HyperStars: sound effects, and interactive music. Since the game has a heavy emphasis on “performance”, the music and crowd reacts to how each player is doing. It builds up as more action is happening and swells into climax if a player makes a comeback.
